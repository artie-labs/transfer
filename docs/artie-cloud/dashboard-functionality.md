# Dashboard Functionality

## Enabling Slack Notifications

You can configure Artie to send alerts to your Slack channel in addition to your email.

1. Navigate to the Slack channel you would like to receive updates in
2. Click on `Integrations`
3. Click on `Send emails to this channel`
4. Copy the email and go to the your `Company Settings` page on Artie
