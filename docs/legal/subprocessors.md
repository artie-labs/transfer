# Subprocessors

Welcome to Artie’s Subprocessor repository page. A subprocessor is any business that customer data may pass through as a side effect of using Artie’s service. We use partners for business processes that are not core to our expertise but are critical to our customers having a quality experience.



| **Name**            | **Related Services**                           |
| ------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Amazon Web Services | Networking and MSK and other hosting services. |
| DigitalOcean        | Networking and compute (DOKS).                 |
